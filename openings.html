What we value

Curiosity

The kind that makes you read the Wikipedia page on how dragonfly compound eyes track prey at 1 a.m., for no reason.

Rigor

When an experiment looks too good, is your first instinct to celebrate, or to look for the bug?

Communication

The best ideas in this lab usually start as someone interrupting a meeting to say "wait, I'm confused."

Ownership

This is your project, not your supervisor's homework. Push back when you disagree.

Patience

Most problems we work on don't yield in a week. You will be stuck, but we are together to figure out.
